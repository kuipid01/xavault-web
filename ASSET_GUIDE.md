# Xavault landing-page asset guide

The current landing page uses a code-built product preview, so it runs without external image assets. When real app screens are ready, replace the preview with the following assets.

## 1. Hero device mockup

- Output: transparent PNG or WebP, at least 2200px wide
- Composition: one front-facing phone and two partially visible angled phones
- Prompt: “Premium fintech mobile app product mockup for a Nigerian salary planning application named Xavault, three modern black smartphones, central phone front-facing with a clean wallet dashboard showing Nigerian naira balance and spending envelopes, side phones angled slightly, deep forest green and warm cream UI, diffused studio lighting, crisp editorial product photography, transparent background, no floating coins, no extra text, no logos besides Xavault”

## 2. Security visual

- Output: transparent PNG/WebP or a short looping WebM
- Prompt: “Minimal 3D secure vault door with subtle fingerprint geometry, dark emerald metal, soft mint rim light, premium fintech aesthetic, centered isolated object, transparent background, no text, no padlock cliché, realistic but understated”

## 3. Social proof portraits

- Output: square portraits, 800×800px minimum
- Direction: use real beta users when possible. Keep lighting and crop consistent. Obtain permission before publishing.

## 4. Open Graph share image

- Output: 1200×630px JPG
- Prompt: “Editorial brand card for Xavault, a Nigerian salary planning fintech, oversized headline ‘Money with somewhere to go.’, deep forest green background, warm cream typography, subtle grid, abstract envelope shapes, minimal premium Swiss-inspired layout, lots of negative space, 1200 by 630”
